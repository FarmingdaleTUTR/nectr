How it works

• The way the payments per sessions are going to be made is by having credit, which are honey-combs. After a certain amount of honey-combs the credit will them become Jars of honey. The tutors have the option to charge how ever many jars of honey or honey-combs per session.

• Every student that they suggest and sign up, the user will receive a free honey-combs. Also after the first session the student will receive a reward of honey-combs. The students can also gain more honey-combs by purchasing a package or by sessions and completing test/quizzes/assignments given by the tutor.
