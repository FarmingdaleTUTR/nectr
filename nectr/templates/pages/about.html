About The Hive

We believe one on one learning is crucial for college students and should be made as easily accessible and convenient as possible. That is why we built nectR.

Learn About Our Story

The creative name nectR came about from the actual word nectar. Nectar has two meanings, first it is a sugary fluid secreted by plants, especially within flowers to encourage pollination by insects and other animals. In addition, in order for bees to produce honey, they collect the fluid. The other definition is that nectar (in Greek and Roman mythology) is the drink of gods. As a group we used this analogy to apply the definitions to our application. We figured out a way to make learning fun and enjoyable for both the student and the tutors. The students are the bees that are looking for fluid to produce honey. The fluid to produce honey is the nectar, which would be the sessions with the tutor, and the honey is the knowledge gained from each session.
